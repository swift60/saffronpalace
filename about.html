About

Hotel Shah Abbas can be very easily reached by anyone and even the tourists who have visited the city for the first time of their lives would also be able to reach the hotel easily. The tourists would not have to trudge the entire city in utter dismay in order to find the hotel. The hotel can be very easily reached by tourists quite effortlessly. Shah Abbas Hotel in Srinagar is located quite conveniently at the Srinagar Boulevard. The Boulevard faces the famous Dal Lake and thus the location of Shah Abbas Hotel in Srinagar would be at a stone's throw distance from this world famous lake. There are regular flights that operate from Delhi and are the best way for reaching Srinagar. Otherwise one can also avail the facility of the Indian railway. There are several places of tourist interest that lie quite close to the location of Shah Abbas Hotel in Srinagar namely Mughal Gardens, Kheer Bhawani, Awantipur, Shankaracharya Temple, Dachigam Sactuary, Gulmarg and Sonamarg.
